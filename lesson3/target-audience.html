Target Audience

The target audience of Real Weather Today are people who love outsides and need information about the weather wherever, and whenever they are. The target audience usually gets into our site on mobile devices.

Two Personas

Persona 1

Young people who enjoy activities outside. The average age is 20 years old. They usually access to internet on mobile devices and like to share photos on social networks. They need weather information to plan their outside activities like long walking on sunny days or snowboarding in the winter.

Persona 2

Mature adults who need to keep informed in every moment, and wherever they are. They like to organize and plan their activities to be more productive. They need weather information to plan their meeting or just an informal lunch. They usually access to internet on their smartphones but also use laptops when they are working. Their average age is 35 years old.

Scenarios

Scenario 1
I have a dinner with my friends tonight. Do I need a jacket?
Scenario 2
I am going to run tomorrow morning. how will be the weather look like?
Scenario 3
I have an important meeting this evening. Is it going to rain?
Scenario 4
I have to walk the dog. Is it too sunny?
Scenario 5
I am planning my coming vacation. I need information to choose clothes.
